Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information on logistics and safety for whitewater rafting. Viewers should be able to easily find answers to common questions about rafting, as well as learn about good safety practices and necessary equipment.

Audience

We expect this site to be used by anyone looking for easy-to-access information about whitewater rafting, specifically appealing to the beginner or the less-experienced. The site might be accessed by users at home computers or laptops doing research for an upcoming or potential trip, or by users on a mobile device when looking for equipment or reviewing safety precautions with a group immediately before a trip begins. The site will strive to meet both of these use cases to ensure the user has a one-stop solution for all their needs.
